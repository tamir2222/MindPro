# Redesign Review Notes

## Visual review of upgraded MindPro homepage

The redesigned homepage is materially stronger than the previous version in its first screen. The hero now communicates a clearer value proposition, a more explicit audience definition, and a stronger conversion path. The sticky header is clean and restrained, the WhatsApp CTA is visible without feeling aggressive, and the hero layout balances the premium athletic image with more concrete professional messaging.

The mid-page structure is also improved. The "למי זה מתאים" section creates clearer audience segmentation, and the subsequent explanatory sections establish a better narrative rhythm than before. The service architecture and authority direction feel more deliberate, which aligns well with the benchmark insight that strong sites in this category move from identity to fit to proof to action.

From the visible sections reviewed so far, the visual language remains original and more clinically controlled than the two benchmark sites. This is positive for a psychologist brand. The overall impression is more persuasive, more mature, and better organized than the earlier version.

## Items to verify next

The lower-page FAQ and contact flow still need final verification for spacing, CTA visibility, and completeness. The final checks should confirm that the WhatsApp CTA remains strong near the bottom of the page and that the authority and service sections preserve good spacing on the way down.
